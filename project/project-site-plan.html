Project Site Plan

This document outlines the site plan for the project.

Site name

EcoExplore Ecuador

This name is short, memorable, and combines “Eco” (ecology, ecosystems) with “Explore,” inviting users to discover Ecuador’s natural richness in an interactive and engaging way.

Site Purpose

The site provides an interactive guide for users and tourists to learn about and explore Ecuador’s national parks. It offers visual and detailed information about each park, including climate, flora, fauna, and available activities. It also includes a contact form to answer inquiries and promote sustainable tourism and environmental conservation.

Escenarios

Which national park is best for hiking and enjoying natural landscapes in Ecuador?
Where can I send an inquiry to get information about tours or activities within a specific park?

Color Scheme

Forest Green (#2E7D32): for headings and highlighted elements, representing nature and vegetation.
Sky Blue (#0288D1): for buttons, links, and accents, evoking sky and water, and providing a fresh feel.
White (#FFFFFF): for the background, ensuring readability and a clean look.

Typography

Montserrat: for headings, due to its modern and clear style that grabs attention.
Open Sans: for paragraph texts and general content, for great screen readability.
